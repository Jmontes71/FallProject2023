Bike Of the Month

Although it’s an entry-level bike, the Diverge E5 is one versatile gravel. Its frame can handle up to 700c x 47mm or 650b x 2.1-inch tires, which make it an off-roading beast, or you can swap to 30mm- or 32 mm-wide rubber tires for a faster, sportier ride. According to Bicycling test editor Dan Chabanov, “the Diverge E5 can be a road bike, gravel bike, drop-bar mountain bike, touring bike, or adventure bike” with a few tweaks of its components. This ride will take you from the dirt to the pavement at a price that matches its comfort.
